Welcome to The 843 paintball team website

The 843 is a paintball team based out of the city of Charleston located in South Carolina. We are a D5 team right now competeing in local tournament series like the Carolina Field Owners Association and the Carolina Premiere Speedball League. Our main goal is to go and compete at a high level of play and have fun while doing it.

"Im not a malicious person, but for some reason the excitement that you get from shooting someone with a paintball is beyond words" -Oliver Lang
