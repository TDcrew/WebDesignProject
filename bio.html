The 843 is a paintball team based out of Charleston, South Carolina. The team was started last year of March 2018. One of my buddys and I had an idea to make a paintball team that would go out and compete at a high level while having fun doing it. This team quickly assembled building a 5-man roster fast to go play in tournaments. Our team being new to the sport wanted to be just like every other team. So, the team started doing research on sponsors to help us get gear and making official team appearal. The team was successful in both apsects. Our first year we landed sponsorships with paintball manufactures Virtue and Gisportz. The team did get one out of region sponsor from a great company called Paragon. Paragon does sitework for contstruction around South Carolina. The last sponsor we got was our local field, Paintball of Charleston. All these sponsors allowed us to get crucial gear we needed like pants, loaders, pod packs and much more. While dealing with the sponsorships the team also worked on making our jerseys for the tournaments we would play in. The team went with a company called Anthrax. Anthrax is known for high quality gear and great customer service. We worked with Anthrax to make our jerseys over about a 2 month period. We got our gear fast from our sponsors like Virtue and Gisportz. Then we received our jerseys later on in the summer.

After setting the team up for the season ahead. We participated in 3 tournaments. The first one was the CFOA 5-man Tarheel Open. The seconod event was the CXL 3-amn #4. The last event was the CFOA Trauma Challenge. The team did not do to hot in any of these tournamnets. We did how ever learn a lot in our first couple of tournaments. After our last tournament we did not really do much in the off season.

At the start of this season we started the team with only 3 players. The season looks bright though. The team is working on finding more people to play with.
