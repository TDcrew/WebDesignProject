What is the purpose of the site?
The purpose of this site is to show off and make a webpage to represent part of a paintball team I manage.
What do you want the web site to accomplish?
I want the website to accomplish the goal of representing our team in a professional manner.
Who is your intended audience?
Our intended audience is other paintball teams, anyone who has a interest in paintball, and any organization that would be willing to sponsor us.
What opportunity, problem, or issue is your site addressing?
The opportunity I see the website taking advantage of is more of a professional aspect for representing the team. Social media only goes so far as representing the team.
What type of content might be included in your site?
Our site would include contact info, photo gallery, event results and a calendar for future events.
